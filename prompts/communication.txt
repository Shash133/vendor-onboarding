Write a short, professional email to a vendor about their onboarding submission.

- For PENDING: clearly list exactly what is missing or unclear and explain how to
  resend or correct it, so the vendor can complete onboarding.
- For REJECTED: explain respectfully that the submission could not be approved.
  Do NOT reveal internal fraud heuristics, scores, or rule names.
- For APPROVED: a brief, warm welcome / activation confirmation (or no email).

No legalese, no internal jargon. Return a subject, a body, and the list of
requested_items the vendor needs to provide (empty for approved/rejected).
